Core principles

Built around four ideas

CounterCore exists to surface the right information at the right moment, without noise, guessing, or dark patterns.

Adaptive

Reads your live match and reacts to the enemy champions and items you can actually see. Recommendations shift as the game evolves.

Explained

Every recommendation comes with a plain-language reason — not just what to build, but why: "Heavy enemy AP — consider magic resist."

Honest

No guessing. When data is unavailable or confidence is low, CounterCore says so explicitly rather than fabricating an answer.

Yours

Works offline once updated. No account required. No tracking. Your match data stays on your machine — it is never sent anywhere.

Under the hood

How CounterCore works

A transparent, display-only overlay that reads what the game already shows you — and turns it into a build plan.

01Step one

Launch alongside League

Start CounterCore before or during champion select. It sits idle until a live match begins, then activates automatically.

02Step two

Reads the live match — locally

CounterCore reads the League Live Client Data API, which runs on your own machine. It sees which enemy champions you are facing and the items they have visibly equipped — nothing more.

03Step three

Computes counter-build recommendations

A bundled counter-knowledge base maps enemy threat profiles to recommended items and runes. Champion and item data come from Riot's official Data Dragon. All processing happens on-device.

04Step four

Shows a transparent overlay

A compact strip shows your top recommendation at a glance. Expand it to see up to four prioritized build suggestions, each with a plain-language explanation. Toggle with a hotkey any time.

  • Display-only — CounterCore does not interact with the game, read game memory, or automate any input.
  • No cooldown timers, no power-spike alerts, no action-dictating notifications.
  • Enemy Item Value shown is the summed gold cost of visible items only — not a prediction of unspent gold.
  • Honest empty states when data is missing — no fabricated confidence.
