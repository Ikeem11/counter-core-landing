Privacy Policy

CounterCore is designed to collect as little data as possible.

No accounts

CounterCore has no login, no user accounts, and no cloud sync. There is nothing to sign up for and no profile to create.

No personal data collection

CounterCore does not collect, store, or transmit your personal information. We do not know who you are, and the application does not attempt to identify you.

Local game data stays local

While a match is active, CounterCore reads League of Legends' Live Client Data API, which runs locally on your own computer. That match data — enemy champion identities, visible item states — is used only on your device to generate recommendations and is never sent to us or to any third party.

Static game assets

CounterCore downloads public champion, item, and rune data and icons from Riot's official Data Dragon content delivery network, and may download an updated recommendation data bundle from our own hosting. These requests fetch public files; they do not send your personal information.

No telemetry required

CounterCore functions fully offline once it has a current data bundle. No background analytics, no usage reporting, no crash telemetry is transmitted without your knowledge.

Future changes

This policy reflects CounterCore's current MVP design. If a future version adds features that change how data is handled — such as an optional premium tier with payment processing, or opt-in analytics — this policy will be revised and the change will be communicated clearly before it takes effect.
